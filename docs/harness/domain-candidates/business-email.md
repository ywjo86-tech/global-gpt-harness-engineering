# Business Email Skill Candidate

## Purpose

Support business email drafting, reply planning, tone adjustment, meeting follow-up, and stakeholder communication.

## Expected Inputs

- Email thread context
- Desired outcome
- Audience
- Constraints or tone requirements

## Expected Outputs

- Draft response
- Reply strategy
- Follow-up actions
- Risk or sensitivity notes

## Creation Trigger

Create this as a real skill only after repeated email workflows require consistent structure, review rules, and safety handling.
